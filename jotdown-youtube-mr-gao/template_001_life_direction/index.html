Seeking Life Direction Template

三部曲 （這裏所有問題一定要想到有為止） Find your life direction in these 3 parts questionnaire, please try your best to answer all of them

價值觀 - 試問自己5個問題？找到關鍵字​

Values, Ask yourself the 5 questions and find the keywords in your answers. You may seek ChatGPT help to come out with some keywords from your answers.

  1. 你有尊敬的人嗎？你為什麼尊敬他？ Who do you respect and why?
  1. 青春期有什麼對你價值觀影響很大的嗎？What happened in your teen that left a strong value anchoring in your life?
  1. 現在社會有什麼不足的地方嗎？What do you think about current society , what is the society lack of?
  1. 你朋友眼中的你是什麼東西？What are you in your friend's eye?
  1. 希望給朋友是麼樣的建議，不希望朋友給什麼建議？ What kind of advice would you give to your friend, what kind of advice do you not want your friend to give you?

Keywords:

才能 - 試問自己5個問題？然後找到關鍵字​

往往人把愛好，技術，技能當成天賦，這些屬於後天可以培養的。這裏指的是非能力因素譬如千杯不倒，長得好看等。 Talent, Ask yourself the 5 questions and find the keywords in your answers. You may seek ChatGPT help to come out with some keywords. Don't mix talent with hobby, skill , hobby and skill can be developed. Talent refers to non-skill related factor such as being good looking.

  1. 人生最充實的經歷是什麼？What is the most enriching experience ?
  1. 最近一次不耐煩的事情是什麼？【對朋友】What is the thing that pissed me off recently?
  1. 問身邊的人自己擅長什麼？ What are you good at in your friend's eye?
  1. 放棄現在工作，特別想做的事情是什麼？What would you do if you were to give up your current career?
  1. 目前成功的事情？What is the successful things for you ?

Keywords:

願望和夢想​

Dreams

  1. 什麼事情就算花錢也願意學？What are the things you would pay for?
  1. 你的書架上有什麼內容的書？What are the contents of book on your bookshelf
  1. 有沒有什麼內容對你人生有幫助？What kind of contents help you in your life?
  1. 你有想感謝的人和事嗎？Who do you want to express your gratitude towards?
  1. 你對社會有什麼不滿？What do you feel bad about current society?
